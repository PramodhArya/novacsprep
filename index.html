WHO WE WERE

NOVACSPREP was a nonprofit dedicated to expanding computer science to everyone. Our goal was to give back to the community by offering everyone various opportunities to dive into the world of computer science through fun and interactive programs without financial barriers. Computer Science will only become increasingly important, and it is vital everyone has access to a high quality education in it.
